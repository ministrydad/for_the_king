The Story

Who is
Matt Hayes?

"It all started on the couch, watching a magician on TV — and my dad leaned over and said, 'I bet you can't do that.'"

Challenge accepted. What started as a simple dare turned into a decade-long passion. Today, through illusion, comedy, and storytelling, Matt creates high-energy experiences that bring people together — and point them to something greater.

Every show is clean, family-friendly, and designed to do more than entertain. It's about creating moments that last — moments that spark wonder, provoke thought, and open hearts.

— Matt Hayes

The Experience

More Than a Magic Show

Every performance weaves together illusion, humor, and faith into something unforgettable.

Grand Illusions

From sleight of hand to stage-scale spectacles, each effect is crafted to spark genuine wonder and leave audiences speechless.

Clean Comedy

Quick wit, audience interaction, and lighthearted humor create an atmosphere where everyone — from kids to grandparents — can relax and laugh.

A Message of Hope

At the center of every performance is a desire to point people to Christ — through stories, metaphors, and moments that reach the heart.

Storytelling

Every illusion is woven into a narrative — connecting tricks to truths, and turning each moment on stage into something deeper and more meaningful.

Everyday to Extravagant

The show builds from intimate close-up moments to jaw-dropping spectacles, keeping the energy rising from the first minute to the last.

Any Audience, Any Venue

Churches, corporate events, schools, private parties — every show is tailored to fit the audience, the space, and the occasion perfectly.
